Privacy Policy

Unofficial Fab Wishlist is a Chrome extension that allows you to save assets from Fab locally in your browser.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Unofficial Fab Wishlist browser extension handles your data.

In short: No personal data is collected. All data—including your wishlist—is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Data Storage

Your wishlist is stored using Chrome's local storage. This data remains on your device and is accessible only by you. Clearing your browser's local storage will clear your wishlist.
